Safety

Safety is at the forefront of everything we do. We have an excellent safety record and actively fund and promote a safety behavioural change programme with our colleagues and subcontractors which has made a significant contribution to the group’s safety culture. From the CEO to our colleagues on the front line, safety is everyone’s responsibility and is the key part of our commitment to the responsible development running through all our activities.
